Planned downtime of dauntless

Published: Mi 13 April 2016
By The Document Foundation

In Status.

Due to the upgrade of our infrastructure, there will be a planned downtime for our virtualization host dauntless, with the following VMs and services affected: - vm138 - crashtest - vm152 - mail-test - vm159 - mailhost-test - vm165 - bibisect repos - vm167 - bitgeria metrics - vm171 - crashreport - vm172 - online help mod - vm173 - hc3 test - vm174 - statistics - win2k12r2

The downtime will be on Wednesday, April 20, 0730 UTC and last for about 30 Minutes.
